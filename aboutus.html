How It Started

We didn't set out to run a gaming network. We just wanted a decent place to play.

For years we hopped between servers looking for the same thing — a place that actually felt like a community. Somewhere with admins who showed up, rules that made sense, and a vibe that wasn't ruined by pay-to-win nonsense.

We never found it. So we built it ourselves.

Zeropoint started as a project between two brothers who wanted to make something genuinely special. A place where people could come to play, unwind, and actually meet other people worth playing with.

We handle every part of this ourselves — the servers, the community, the moderation, the events. No company behind it, no investors. Just two guys who love games and care about the experience you have here.

"We used to play on other people's servers and weren't happy with the service — so we made our own." — 𝐍𝐲𝐫𝐢𝐱ツ & Duke, Co-Founders
01

We were players first

We didn't start this to make money. We started it because we spent years playing on servers that let us down. We know exactly what a bad experience feels like — and we built Zeropoint to be the opposite of that.

02

Community over profit

No pay-to-win. No fake scarcity. No absent admins. Every decision we make starts with one question: is this actually good for the people playing here?

03

We're still here

We're not absentee owners. We play on our own servers. We're in the Discord. If something's wrong, you can talk to us directly — no ticket system, no bot runaround.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Three things we refuse to compromise on, no matter how the network grows.

Player First, Always

We play on our own servers. Every rule, setting, and decision is made by people who actually experience the impact firsthand.

Zero Pay-to-Win

No advantages for sale. No premium tiers that make the game unfair. What you earn in-game, you earn by playing — not spending.

Real Community

We're not running a service. We're building a community. Admins are active members, not just moderators. You can always reach us directly.
