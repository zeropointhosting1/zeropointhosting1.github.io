Server Rules

Read and follow these rules to keep your account and base safe. Violations may result in dino wipes, base wipes, or bans.

⚠️
These rules are enforced. Failure to follow them may result in wipes, dino wipes, or a permanent ban. When in doubt, ask an admin in Discord before doing something you're unsure about.
Player Names & Conduct
🚫Generic names like Human or Bob are not allowed
🚫Symbol-only or gibberish names are not allowed
🚫Cussing or profanity in names or chat
🚫Bullying or harassment of any player
🚫Discussing players' real lives or private information
🔐Zero tolerance policy — player safety comes first.
Tribes
✅Tribes are required on every map
⚠️Insiding will not be handled by admins — tribe management is your responsibility
Base Rules
🏠1 base per tribe
✅Bases may be on land, water, or used as a shop
🌊Water bases must be in open water and contain 80% water dinos
✅Normal caves are allowed
❌No building in artifact caves
❌No building in wyvern scars
❌No building near beaver dams
❌No building at player spawn points
❌No blocking caves or map progression
Moving Bases
🪧Place a sign outside your base saying "MOVING BASE"
🎟️Or open an admin ticket in Discord
🧨Your old base must be fully demolished after moving
Building Distance Rules
🚫Do not build outside your own render
🚫Do not build within another player's render
❓If unsure, ask an admin first before placing
Dino Limits
💤Offline: Max 35 dinos out
🟢Online: Max 55 dinos out
❌Offline breeding is strictly forbidden
❌Mass breeding is strictly forbidden
❌No dinos left on wandering
Structures & Spam
❌Structure spam is not allowed
🧹Abandoned traps will be wiped by admins without warning
Structure Limits (Per Base)
🧬Max 3 Cloners
🔧Max 3 Replicators or Meks
🥚Max 4 Egg Incubators
⚡No Tek Generator pulse
Teleporters
🌐A public teleporter at your base is required
🔓Public TP must remain open at all times
🚫Public TP cannot be enclosed
Abandoned Wipe Policy
🦖Abandoned dinos will be wiped
🏚️Bases left inactive for 7+ days will be wiped

✨ Be respectful  ·  Play fair  ·  Have fun and enjoy the grind 🦖🔥

Questions? Open a ticket in our Discord server.
