About

What is Zeropoint?

Zeropoint was started by two brothers who share a passion for building something people actually enjoy being part of. We host our own servers, handle our own community, and stay hands-on because we genuinely care about the experience — not just keeping the lights on.

No corpo nonsense. No pay-to-win. Just a well-run server with people who play alongside you. Hytale is now live — join us at play.zeropointhosting.com.
