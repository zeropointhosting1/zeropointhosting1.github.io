About

What is Zeropoint?

Zeropoint is a community built around shared survival experiences. We started as a group of ARK players who wanted a server that actually felt like home — no pay-to-win, fair rules, and admins who play alongside you.

As Hytale approaches, we're expanding. Our goal is to be the community you join on day one and stay in for years.
