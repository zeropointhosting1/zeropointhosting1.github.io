FAQ
When will I receive my rewards?
All in-game rewards are delivered at or shortly after Hytale's official launch. You'll be notified via the email you provide when ordering. Your Discord roles are granted within 24 hours of confirmed payment.
What if Hytale's release keeps getting delayed?
We're in it for the long haul. Your supporter role and Discord perks — like the early access role and badge — are granted immediately after payment is confirmed. In-game rewards activate at launch, whenever that happens.
Are these packages refundable?
All sales are final as these are community support donations. In the unlikely event that Hytale is cancelled entirely, we'll reach out via Discord to discuss options on a case-by-case basis.
How do I get my Discord role after donating?
After completing your order, join our Discord server and open a support ticket or message an admin with your 4-digit tracking code. We'll assign your role within 24 hours of verifying the payment.
Can I upgrade my tier later?
Yes — just purchase the higher tier and message us in Discord. We'll apply the upgrade and update your role accordingly.
